---
title: "Import & Export SOPs"
description: "Move SOPs in and out of your organisation."
---

The **Import/Export** tab in Organisation Settings lets you move SOPs as portable JSON bundles — useful for backups, migrations, or sharing procedures between organisations.

<Frame caption="Import and export SOPs">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/org-import-export.png" alt="Import and export SOPs" />
</Frame>

## Export SOPs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Import/Export">
    In Organisation Settings, select the **Import/Export** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what to export">
    Export **all** SOPs, or select specific ones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the bundle">
    A JSON file downloads to your device (named for your organisation).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Import SOPs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your file">
    Choose a previously exported JSON bundle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a conflict strategy">
    Decide what happens when a title already exists:
    - **Create new** — always add as a new SOP.
    - **Skip if title exists** — leave existing ones untouched.
    - **Update if title exists** — update fields and tags on a match.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose whether to include steps">
    Include the full step content, or import titles/metadata only.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the import">
    Process Central imports the SOPs according to your choices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Choose your conflict strategy carefully — **Update if title exists** overwrites fields and tags on matching SOPs. Export a backup first if you're unsure.
</Warning>
