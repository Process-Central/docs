The **Automation** tab in Organisation Settings is where you connect the automation platforms your SOPs trigger — such as **n8n**, **ActivePieces** and **Rundeck**. Once connected, a step can fire a workflow and the response is recorded against the execution.

  <img src="/images/screenshots/org-automation.png" alt="Automation provider configuration" />
</Frame>

## Connect a provider

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Automation tab">
    Organisation switcher → **Organisation Settings** → **Automation**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your platform">
    Select the automation platform you run (e.g. n8n).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the connection details">
    Provide the endpoint URL and API key/credentials for your instance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and save">
    Use **Test** to confirm connectivity, then save the configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Configuring an n8n automation provider">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/AutomationProvider.png" alt="n8n automation provider configuration" />
</Frame>

Enter a **Configuration Name**, the **API URL** and **API Key** for your instance, then **Test** — a successful test reports how many workflows were found. Tick **Set as default configuration** and **Save Configuration**.

## How it's used during execution

When an SOP (or step) with an automation rule runs, Process Central triggers the linked workflow and records the outcome. The response appears under the step's **Expected Result** during execution, and in [Audit & Evidence](/admin/audit-evidence) afterwards.

<Note>
Automation is a plan-dependent capability. If you don't see the **Automation** tab, check your subscription tier.
</Note>
