The **Organisation** tab in Organisation Settings holds your core organisation details and branding.

<Frame caption="Organisation details">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/org-organisation.png" alt="Organisation details settings" />
</Frame>

## Open organisation settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the organisation switcher">
    Select your organisation name at the top of the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Organisation Settings">
    This opens the settings with the **Organisation** tab selected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit your details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Update the basics">
    Set your organisation **name** and **domain**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add branding">
    Upload your **logo** — it appears in the sidebar and on your organisation's SOP exports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your branding applies across the app for everyone in the organisation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
A recognisable logo and consistent name help your team trust that they're in the right workspace — especially when people belong to more than one organisation.
</Tip>
