**Prompt Management** lets administrators review and refine the prompts Process Central uses for its AI-assisted features, so AI output matches your organisation's expectations.

  <img src="/images/screenshots/prompt-management.png" alt="Prompt management" />
</Frame>

## Manage prompts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Prompt Management">
    Go to **Settings → Prompts** (`/settings/prompts`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the prompts">
    See the prompts used across AI-assisted features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust and save">
    Refine a prompt's wording to better fit your terminology and standards, then save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Small, deliberate wording changes usually beat large rewrites. Adjust one prompt, observe the results, then iterate.
</Tip>

<Note>
Prompt management works together with your [AI & LLM configuration](/admin/ai-configuration).
</Note>
