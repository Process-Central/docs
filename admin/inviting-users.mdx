Use the **Invite Users** tab in Organisation Settings to add people to your organisation.

<Frame caption="Inviting users">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/org-invite-users.png" alt="Invite users" />
</Frame>

## Send an invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Invite Users">
    In Organisation Settings, select the **Invite Users** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the email">
    Type the person's email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a role">
    Pick the role that fits — **Viewer**, **Member**, **Manager** or **Admin**. You can change it later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Select **Send Invite**. The person receives an email with a link to set their password and join.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the invited user sees

They'll open the invitation link, set a password, and land in your organisation. See [Signing in](/getting-started/signing-in) for the new-user flow.

<Note>
Your plan sets how many users your organisation can have. Check your current count against the limit on the [Dashboard](/user-guide/dashboard). To add more, see [Subscription & billing](/admin/subscription-billing).
</Note>

<Tip>
Grant the least access needed to start. It's easy to promote someone later under [Managing users](/admin/managing-users).
</Tip>
