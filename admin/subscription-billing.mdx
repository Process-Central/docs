The **Subscription** tab in Organisation Settings is where owners and admins manage the plan, start trials and access billing.

  <img src="/images/screenshots/org-subscription.png" alt="Subscription and billing" />
</Frame>

## Manage your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Subscription">
    In Organisation Settings, select the **Subscription** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your current plan">
    See your tier, what's included, and any active trial and its remaining days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upgrade or start a trial">
    Move to a higher tier, or start a trial to unlock features like Templates, Process Maps and Agentic Ops.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the billing portal">
    Manage payment details and invoices through the secure billing portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Only **administrators** and **owners** can change the subscription or start trials. Billing is handled securely through Stripe.
</Note>

## Plans and limits

Your plan governs the number of **users** and **SOPs**, storage, and which features are available. Track your usage against these limits on the [Dashboard](/user-guide/dashboard).
