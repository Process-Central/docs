<Note>
**Audit & Evidence** is available to administrators and owners.
</Note>

This is where documentation meets proof. **Audit & Evidence** gives you the defensible record that your SOPs were actually followed — who ran them, when, and the outcome — ready for an internal or external audit.

## Review the audit trail

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Audit & Evidence">
    Select **Audit &amp; Evidence** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review executions">
    See the record of SOP executions, including the person or agent, timestamp and outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drill into the evidence">
    Open an entry to see the captured evidence for each step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using evidence for compliance

Standards such as **ISO 9001**, **ISO 27001** and **ISO 22000 / HACCP** ask for two things: documented procedures, and objective evidence they are followed. Process Central covers both:

| The standard asks for… | Where it lives in Process Central |
| --- | --- |
| Documented procedures | [SOPository](/user-guide/sopository) |
| The right people trained on them | [Assignments](/admin/assignments) |
| Controlled execution | [Executing SOPs](/user-guide/executing-sops) |
| Monitoring & measurement | [Reporting](/user-guide/reporting) |
| Objective evidence for the auditor | **Audit & Evidence** |

<Tip>
Because AI agents run under the same procedures and trail as people, the audit record covers *all* your work — human and automated — in one place.
</Tip>
