<Note>
**Assignments** is an admin/owner feature on selected plans. If you don't see it in the sidebar, check your role and subscription tier.
</Note>

**Assignments** is where you allocate work — mapping SOPs to the people and teams responsible for them, so ownership and accountability are clear.

## Allocate an SOP

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assignments">
    Select **Assignments** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the SOP">
    Pick the procedure you want to allocate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign people or teams">
    Allocate it to the individuals or teams responsible for carrying it out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Assignees now have clear ownership, and their execution feeds the audit record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Assign to a **team** rather than an individual where a role — not a specific person — owns the procedure. It keeps working even as people change.
</Tip>
