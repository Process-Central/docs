title: "Assignments"

<Note>
**Assignments** is an admin/owner feature on selected plans. If you don't see it in the sidebar, check your role and subscription tier.
</Note>

**Groups & Assignments** is where you allocate work — group people together, then assign SOPs (or all SOPs with a given tag) to a group on a schedule, so ownership and accountability are clear.

  <img src="/images/screenshots/GroupsandAssignments.png" alt="Groups and assignments" />
</Frame>

## Create a group

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assignments">
    Select **Assignments** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a group">
    In the **Groups** panel, select **+ New group** and give it a name (e.g. "Back Office").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add members">
    Edit the group to add the people who belong to it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create an assignment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new assignment">
    In the **Assignments** panel, select **+ New assignment**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what to assign">
    Assign a single SOP, or **all SOPs with a tag** (e.g. everything tagged "Office Tasks").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose who it goes to">
    Assign **to a group** (or an individual), then pick the group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the schedule">
    Choose a schedule — for example **One-off** or a recurring cadence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    The SOPs are now allocated, and completions feed the audit record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Assigning by **tag** to a **group** keeps working as procedures and people change — new SOPs with that tag are automatically covered, and group membership can be updated without redoing the assignment.
</Tip>
