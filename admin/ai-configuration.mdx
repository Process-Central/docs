The **AI & LLM** tab in Organisation Settings is where administrators configure the AI capabilities your organisation uses.

<Frame caption="AI & LLM configuration">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/org-ai-llm.png" alt="AI and LLM configuration" />
</Frame>

## Configure AI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI & LLM">
    In Organisation Settings, select the **AI & LLM** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your model">
    Configure the AI/LLM provider and settings for your organisation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your configuration applies to AI-assisted features across the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Related: prompts

Fine-tune how the AI behaves by managing the prompts it uses. See [Prompt management](/admin/prompt-management).

<Note>
AI features and available providers may depend on your subscription tier.
</Note>
