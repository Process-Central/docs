Process Central is where your organisation's **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)**, automation and AI agents live together — documented, executed and provable under a single audit trail.

This documentation is split into two parts:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User Guide" icon="user" href="/user-guide/dashboard">
    Day-to-day use: finding, writing, executing and tracking SOPs and tasks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Administration" icon="gear" href="/admin/overview">
    Owner and admin functions: users, roles, billing, AI config and evidence.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What you can do with Process Central

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Document SOPs" icon="file-lines">
    Build step-by-step procedures with instructions, screenshots and expected results in the **SOPository**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Execute with evidence" icon="clipboard-check">
    Run SOPs step-by-step and capture proof that each one was actually followed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Allocate work" icon="users">
    Assign SOPs and tasks to the right people and teams with clear ownership.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Govern automation & AI" icon="robot">
    Bring automation playbooks and AI agents under the same SOPs and audit trail as your people.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who this is for

- **Team members** who run procedures and complete tasks day to day.
- **Managers** who allocate work and monitor performance.
- **Administrators and owners** who configure the organisation, manage users, and produce evidence for audits.

<Note>
Some areas — such as **Templates**, **Process Maps** and **Agentic Ops** — depend on your subscription tier. **Assignments** and **Audit & Evidence** are only visible to administrators and owners. If you can't see a screen mentioned here, check your plan or ask your administrator.
</Note>
