Your personal **Settings** control your profile, how the app looks and behaves for you, and your account security. Open them from your avatar in the top bar or footer, then **Settings**.

<Frame caption="Personal settings — profile">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/settings-profile.png" alt="Profile settings" />
</Frame>

## Profile information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Select your avatar, then **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your details">
    Edit your name, contact details and avatar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your changes apply across the app immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Preferences

<Frame caption="Preferences">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/settings-preferences.png" alt="Preferences settings" />
</Frame>

- **Theme** — choose light or dark mode (also available from the sidebar footer).
- **Restart Welcome Tour** — replay the guided introduction to the app.
- Organisation-related preferences that apply to you.

## Security

Manage your password and account security from the **Security** section of Settings.

<Tip>
Use a strong, unique password. If your organisation offers it, keep **Remember me** only for devices you personally control.
</Tip>
