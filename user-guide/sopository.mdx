The **SOPository** is your organisation's central library of Standard Operating Procedures. Every SOP lives here, organised and searchable.

<Frame caption="The SOPository — your SOP library">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/sopository.png" alt="The SOPository SOP list" />
</Frame>

## Find an SOP

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the SOPository">
    Select **SOPority** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search or filter">
    Use the search box to find an SOP by title, or filter by **status** (Draft, Review, Published, Archived) and **tags**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open it">
    Select an SOP to view its details and steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding SOP status

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Being written — not yet ready for use. |
| **Review** | Awaiting approval before publishing. |
| **Published** | Live and available for execution. |
| **Archived** | Retired, kept for the record. |

## Browse by category

The SOPository groups procedures into category cards. Select a card — for example **Operations** — to drill into just that category's SOPs.
