**Reporting** turns your SOP executions and tasks into insight — so you can see what's working, spot patterns, and identify where your team needs support.

  <img src="/images/screenshots/reporting.png" alt="Process Central reporting" />
</Frame>

## What reporting shows

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Reporting">
    Select **Reporting** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review performance">
    See completion rates, activity and trends across your SOPs and tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spot patterns">
    Identify strengths and areas that need attention — for example procedures that are frequently missed or delayed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use reporting to improve

- Recognise teams and individuals who consistently follow procedures.
- Find bottlenecks and refine SOPs that are hard to complete.
- Demonstrate performance and accountability to stakeholders.

<Note>
For formal, audit-grade evidence that specific SOPs were executed — including who, when and the outcome — administrators can use [Audit & Evidence](/admin/audit-evidence).
</Note>
