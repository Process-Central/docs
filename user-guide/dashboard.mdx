The **Dashboard** is your home screen. It summarises how your organisation is using Process Central and gives you shortcuts to the things you do most.

<Frame caption="The Process Central dashboard">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/dashboard.png" alt="Process Central dashboard" />
</Frame>

## What you'll see

<Steps>
  <Step title="Current usage">
    Live counts for **SOPs**, **Users** and **Storage** against your plan limits — so you always know where you stand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recent SOPs">
    The most recently updated procedures, with their status (Draft, Review, Published, Archived). Select any to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quick actions">
    One-click shortcuts such as **Create New SOP**, **Manage Team** and **Organisation Settings**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
