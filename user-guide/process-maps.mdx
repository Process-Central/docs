<Note>
Process Maps are part of the automation capability and are available on selected plans. If you don't see **Process Maps** in the sidebar, check your subscription tier.
</Note>

**Process Maps** give you a visual view of how your procedures and automation fit together — so manual and automated steps are considered as one connected process rather than isolated silos.

## Work with process maps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Process Maps">
    Select **Process Maps** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the map">
    See how SOPs, tasks and automation link together across a process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the flow">
    Trace where a process starts, which steps are manual, and where automation or AI agents take over.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Mapping manual and automated steps together makes hand-offs obvious — and shows exactly where evidence needs to be captured.
</Tip>
