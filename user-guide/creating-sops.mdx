## Generate an SOP with AI

The fastest way to start is the **AI SOP Generator** — describe what you want and it drafts a complete SOP with steps for you to review and refine.

<Frame caption="The AI SOP Generator">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/SOPAIGenerator.png" alt="AI SOP Generator dialog" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the generator">
    Select the AI generate option when creating a new SOP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe what you want">
    Be specific about the process, tools and desired outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set category and difficulty">
    Choose a **Category** and a **Difficulty Level** (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Include automation suggestions (optional)">
    Tick this to have the AI suggest where automation or agentic AI could help.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate SOP">
    Review the generated steps, then edit and publish as normal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
AI generation requires an [AI provider](/admin/ai-configuration) to be configured for your organisation.
</Note>

## Create a new SOP manually

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new SOP">
    Select **+ New SOP** in the top bar, or **Create New SOP** on the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the basics">
    Give it a clear **title** and a short **description** of its purpose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add steps">
    Break the procedure into ordered **steps**. For each step, include:
    - **Instructions** — what to do.
    - **Screenshots** — what it should look like.
    - **Expected result** — how to know the step is done correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag it">
    Add [tags](/user-guide/tags) so the SOP is easy to find and group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save as a **Draft** while you work. It won't be available for execution until published.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit an existing SOP

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the SOP">
    Find it in the [SOPository](/user-guide/sopository) and open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Edit">
    Make your changes to steps, instructions or tags.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Updates are captured so the record always reflects the current procedure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Publishing workflow

Move an SOP through its lifecycle as it matures:

**Draft → Review → Published → Archived**

<Tip>
Keep each step to a single, clear action. Short, well-illustrated steps are easier to follow and produce cleaner execution evidence.
</Tip>
