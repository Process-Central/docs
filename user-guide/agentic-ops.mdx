title: "Agentic Ops"
description: "Manage AI skills and agent tasks alongside your human procedures."
---

<Note>
Agentic Ops is available on selected plans. If you don't see **Agentic Ops** in the sidebar, check your subscription tier.
</Note>

**Agentic Ops** is where your AI agents are managed and linked to the processes your business uses — under the same SOPs and audit trail as your people. It has two areas: **Skills** and **Agent Tasks**.

## Skills

Skills are reusable capabilities your AI agents can run — defined, versioned and shared in one place so every agent works from the same trusted playbook.

<Frame caption="Agentic Skills">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/agentic-skills.png" alt="Agentic skills" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agentic Ops → Skills">
    Expand **Agentic Ops** in the sidebar and select **Skills**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review available skills">
    See the skills defined for your organisation and what each one does.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage a skill">
    Create, update or link skills to the SOPs and processes they support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Agent Tasks

Agent Tasks are the units of work your AI agents carry out — visible and governed alongside human tasks.

<Frame caption="Agent Tasks">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/agentic-tasks.png" alt="Agent tasks" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agentic Ops → Agent Tasks">
    Expand **Agentic Ops** in the sidebar and select **Agent Tasks**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor agent work">
    Track what your agents are doing, their status and outcomes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep it governed">
    Because agent work runs under the same procedures and trail as people, you get one consistent record of everything that happened.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Connect the coding and automation agents your teams already use (such as opencode, Codex and Claude Code) so both people and agents run from — and are measured against — the same SOPs.
</Tip>
