title: "Tag management"
description: "Categorise and organise your SOPs so they're easy to find."
---

**Tags** keep your growing SOP library navigable. Use them to group procedures by team, location, process area, standard, or anything else that fits how you work.

<Frame caption="Tag management">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/tags.png" alt="Managing tags" />
</Frame>

## Manage tags

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Tag Management">
    Select **Tag Management** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a tag">
    Add a new tag with a clear, consistent name (for example `Onboarding`, `Food Safety`, `Finance`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or remove">
    Rename tags to keep your taxonomy tidy, or remove ones you no longer need.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Apply tags to SOPs

Add tags while [creating or editing an SOP](/user-guide/creating-sops). Once tagged, you can filter the [SOPository](/user-guide/sopository) by tag to quickly narrow down to the procedures you need.

<Tip>
Agree a small, consistent set of tags across your team before you scale up. A tidy tag list is far more useful than dozens of one-off labels.
</Tip>
