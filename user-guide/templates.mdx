<Note>
Templates are available on selected plans. If you don't see **Templates** in the sidebar, check your subscription tier with your administrator.
</Note>

The **Template Gallery** gives you a head start on common procedures, so you're editing rather than starting from a blank page.

## Use a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Templates">
    Select **Templates** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the gallery">
    Review the available templates and pick one that fits the procedure you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create from template">
    Start a new SOP pre-filled with the template's structure and steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise">
    Edit the steps, instructions and tags to match how your organisation actually works, then save and publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Templates are a starting point, not a finish line — always tailor the steps and expected results to your own environment before publishing.
</Tip>
