## Work your tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Tasks">
    Select **Tasks** in the sidebar to see what's assigned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the details">
    Open a task to see its description, due date, and any linked SOP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Do the work">
    Follow any linked procedure and complete the task.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark it complete">
    Update the status so your team has an accurate picture of progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Stay on schedule

- Tasks can carry **due dates** so nothing slips.
- **Reminders and alerts** help you and your team keep to schedule.
- Progress is visible to managers through [Reporting](/user-guide/reporting).

<Tip>
Link a task to an SOP so whoever picks it up has the exact procedure to follow — and their completion becomes part of the execution record.
</Tip>
