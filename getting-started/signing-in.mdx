Process Central is a web application — you can use it from any modern browser on any device.

  <img src="/images/screenshots/signin.png" alt="Process Central sign-in page" />

## Sign in to your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Go to [app.processcentral.co.nz](https://app.processcentral.co.nz).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Type the **email address** and **password** for your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) stay signed in">
    Tick **Remember me** on a trusted device to keep your session active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Sign in">
    You'll land on your **Dashboard**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First time here?

If your administrator has invited you, you'll receive an email with a link to set your password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invitation email">
    Select the link to accept your invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your password">
    Choose a strong password and confirm it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use your new password to sign in for the first time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Forgotten your password? Select **Forgot password?** on the sign-in screen and follow the reset email.
</Tip>

## New users and the welcome tour

The first time you reach the Dashboard, Process Central offers a short **welcome tour** that points out the key areas of the app. You can restart it any time from **Settings → Preferences → Restart Welcome Tour**.
