## The sidebar

At the top of the sidebar is your **organisation switcher**. Below it are the main areas:

| Item | What it's for |
| --- | --- |
| **Dashboard** | Your overview of usage, recent SOPs and quick actions. |
| **SOPository** | The library of all your Standard Operating Procedures. |
| **Tasks** | Operational tasks assigned to you and your team. |
| **Tag Management** | Organise and categorise SOPs with tags. |
| **Templates** | Pre-built SOP templates *(plan dependent)*. |
| **Governance** | The policies, standards, objectives and risks your SOPs deliver against *(plan dependent)*. |
| **Reporting** | Performance, patterns and completion reporting. |
| **Process Maps** | Visual maps linking processes and automation *(plan dependent)*. |
| **Agentic Ops** | Manage AI **Skills** and **Agent Tasks** *(plan dependent)*. |
| **Assignments** | Allocate SOPs to people and teams *(admin/owner)*. |
| **Audit & Evidence** | Proof that SOPs were used and the outcomes *(admin/owner)*. |

<Note>
Items marked *plan dependent* appear based on your subscription tier. **Assignments** and **Audit & Evidence** are only shown to administrators and owners.
</Note>

## Top bar quick actions

- **+ New SOP** — start a new procedure from anywhere.
- **Theme toggle** — switch between light and dark mode.
- **Your avatar** — open **Settings**, privacy, feedback, or **Sign out**.

## Getting help

- **SOPport** in the sidebar opens support options.
- **Changelog** shows what's new in each release.
- Reach the team any time at [support@processcentral.co.nz](mailto:support@processcentral.co.nz).
